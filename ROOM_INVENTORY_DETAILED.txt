METER TRACKING METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

Data Points Tracked Per Room/Month:
1. Room Identifier (ห้อง)
2. Electricity Current Reading (ไฟล่าสุด)
3. Electricity Previous Reading (ไฟเดิม)
4. Electricity Usage (Difference)
5. Electricity Cost (Usage × 8.0 Baht)
6. Water Current Reading (น้ำล่าสุด)
7. Water Previous Reading (น้ำเดิม)
8. Water Usage (Difference)
9. Water Cost (Usage × 20.0 Baht)
10. Monthly Rent (ค่าห้อง)
11. Garbage Fee (ขยะ = 20 Baht fixed)
12. Total Monthly Bill (Rent + Electricity + Water + Garbage)

Usage Calculation Formula:
  Usage = Current Month Reading - Previous Month Reading
  Cost = Usage × Unit Price

Example Calculation (Room 13, March Year 69):
  Electricity: (3,291 - 3,267) × 8 = 24 × 8 = 192 Baht
  Water: (1,710 - 1,707) × 20 = 3 × 20 = 60 Baht
  Total Bill: 1,500 (rent) + 192 (electricity) + 60 (water) + 20 (garbage) = 1,772 Baht

Example Calculation (Large Shop, March Year 69):
  Rent: 15,000 Baht
  Electricity: (20,839 - 20,437) × 8 = 402 × 8 = 3,216 Baht
  Water: (1,364 - 1,358) × 20 = 6 × 20 = 120 Baht
