========================================================================================================================
CALCULATION LOGIC
========================================================================================================================

WATER CHARGES:
  Column B (Previous Water Reading) → F (Formula =B)
  Column G (Water Meter Previous Value)
  Column H (Formula =F-G) = Water Usage
  Column I (Rate: 8 baht/unit)
  Column J (Formula =H*I) = WATER COST

ELECTRIC CHARGES:
  Column C (Previous Electric Reading) → L (Formula =C)
  Column M (Electric Meter Previous Value)
  Column N (Formula =L-M) = Electric Usage
  Column O (Rate: 20 baht/unit)
  Column P (Formula =N*O) = ELECTRIC COST

RENT & FEES:
  Column D = RENT (fixed amount, e.g., 1500)
  Column S = TRASH/COMMON FEE (fixed amount, e.g., 20)

TOTAL:
  Column R (Formula =J+P+D) = Subtotal
  Column T (Formula =R+S) = GRAND TOTAL
